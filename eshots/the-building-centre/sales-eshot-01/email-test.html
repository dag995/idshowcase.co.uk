What is the Interior Design Showcase and what does it do?

Provides regular mailings of product information to 6000 hand-selected designers and architects including:-

  • multi-disciplinary design firms - usually specialising in retail interiors
  • independent interior design companies
  • interior designers within architectural practices
  • interior designers working within retail, hotel and property development chains
  • yacht interior designers
  • multi-disciplinary construction-related firms

Designed to

  • Provide clear, succinct information and key data relevant to busy designers
  • Generate enquiries for your product using relevant calls to action
  • Provide a dedicated landing page for your product
  • Keep all product information available online to view at any time

Open rates

Typically, we expect an open rate of between 25 and 30% and a click-through rate of 10% (where recipient clicks through to landing page). These figures apply to responses within seven days, however typically enquiries will continue to be generated as the eshot will be saved for future reference.
